What is the cost of founder dependency for Australian SMEs?

The Cost of Staying Stuck Calculator helps Australian small business owners quantify how much founder dependency is costing them — in real dollars, per year. Built by Simkul Solutions, a Perth-based SME consulting firm, this free tool takes two inputs: the hours per week a business owner spends on tasks someone else could handle, and their approximate hourly rate.

Most SME owners who run the calculation find the annual figure is significantly higher than expected. At 15 hours per week and $100 per hour, that's $78,000 per year in founder time spent on tasks that should be delegated, documented, or handed to an offshore hire.
