Free operations health check for Australian SME owners

The Simkul Solutions Operations Health Score is a free 12-question diagnostic for Australian small business owners. It assesses four operational dimensions — delegation, documentation, systems, and team capability — and produces a scored result out of 100 with a breakdown by dimension. Business owners receive a plain-English explanation of their weakest area and specific guidance on what to address first.

Most Australian SMEs with 5 to 50 staff have at least one operational dimension scoring below 50 out of 100. The most common weakness is documentation — key processes that exist only in the owner's head, making delegation and scaling significantly harder than it needs to be. Simkul Solutions is a Perth-based consulting firm helping Australian SME owners fix the operational foundations that hold businesses back from growing.
